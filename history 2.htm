HISTORY

Men have been captivated by legends of old, have retold them in different ways, elaborating some and neglecting others, have sought different beauties and values in them, and, when they gave them different conscious interpretations, have educed from them different kinds of truths.

The Philippines is rich in folklore.
By folklore is meant the oral literature of a people usually found among the masses.
It includes traditions, customs, fairy tales, ballads, songs, accounts of ancient festivals, names, superstitions, and beliefs, proverbs, popular sayings, nicknames, nursery rhymes, riddles and jingles of every sort.

Knowledge of a nation's foklore is knowledge of the creative workings of the minds of its folk.
It is a key to a nation's values, a path that leads into the heart of people.
Toledo is rich with folklore.
